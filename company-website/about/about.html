About Hyrule Fields

Since 1986, The Legend of Zelda® has captivated our minds and ignited our imagination for gaming. In 2017, Allen and Dawn decided to create Hyrule Fields to provide something special to other fans of The Legend of Zelda® game series.

Our two person team works to procure decorative art for fans to display in their homes and places of work. Each piece selected here at Hyrule Fields must meet strict standards for shape and form while maintaining the heart of the game series.

We hope you find something unique and special here on our site that can express your own dedication to The Legend of Zelda®!
